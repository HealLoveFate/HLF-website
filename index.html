FAQ

What are Sound Healing and Reiki?
They guide attention from external performance toward internal alignment and balance.Learn practice love and self-discovery
What is the core value of these practices?
Immersive sessions on the inner work that shapes our relationships. Understand how somatic healing, attachment patterns, and self-compassion create new possibilities for love in your life.
Who is this suitable for?
It is suited for those seeking a quiet space to reconnect within — also individuals in high-focus, fast-paced environments seeking intentional stillness.
Is it normal to experience emotions or imagery?
Yes, it is part of inner awareness and varies for each individual.
Why does each session feel different?
Each session is shaped by your present state — your energy, emotions, and readiness on that particular day. Sound and Reiki respond to what is alive in you at that moment, so the experience naturally shifts each time. No two sessions are identical, and that variation is part of the practice itself.
How is sound healing different from music?
Music is experienced primarily through listening, often engaging memory, emotion, and thought. Sound healing works through frequency and resonance — tones are applied intentionally to invite the nervous system into stillness, not to entertain. The aim is presence rather than enjoyment.
How often should I attend sessions?
It depends on your rhythm. Some people benefit from regular weekly or bi-weekly sessions as an ongoing practice, while others come occasionally for a reset or during a period of transition. There is no single right answer — trust what your body and schedule need.
Is it normal to feel emotional, and can this replace medical care?
In a relaxed state, inner emotions may gently surface — this is natural and welcomed as part of the process. That said, sound healing and Reiki are experience-based practices focused on relaxation, awareness, and state alignment. They are not a substitute for medical care or diagnosis. For any health-related concerns, professional medical advice is always recommended.

A Few Things to Know

Not a Medical Substitute

Sound healing and Reiki are not substitutes for medical care. If you are currently receiving medical treatment, please consult your doctor first — and we warmly invite you to share your situation with us before booking.

Open to Everyone

Our services are not affiliated with any religion or belief system. Everyone is warmly welcomed. Group sessions are announced periodically on our social media — follow us to stay updated.

What to Wear

We recommend loose, comfortable clothing so your body can fully relax. Avoid tight or restrictive outfits. You may also bring a light blanket or eye mask if you wish.

After Your Session

Drink plenty of water afterward and give your body time to integrate. Feeling tired or emotional is completely natural — allow yourself to rest and receive with gentleness.

Your Feedback Matters

We warmly welcome your thoughts after each session. Your sharing helps us grow and serve you better. Feel free to reach out anytime.

Special Health Conditions

If you are pregnant, have a heart condition, or any other health concern, please let us know when booking so we can best support and care for you.
